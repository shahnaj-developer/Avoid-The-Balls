Help & Support

Welcome to the Dodge-and-Collect Help Center. We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you might have. Explore the topics below or contact us directly.

Getting Started

  • How do I play Dodge-and-Collect? Dodge obstacles and collect rewards to get the highest score possible. The longer you survive, the higher your score!
  • What devices are supported? Dodge-and-Collect is available on most modern mobile devices and browsers.
  • Do I need an account? No account is required to play, but creating one allows you to save progress and compete on leaderboards.

Technical Support

  • The game won’t load or crashes — what do I do? Try refreshing your browser or restarting the app. Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
  • How do I report bugs or glitches? Please email us at tahsin.abc2009@gmail.com with details of the issue and your device info.
  • Can I play offline? Currently, Dodge-and-Collect requires an internet connection to play.

Account & Privacy

  • How do I create or delete my account? You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button in the app. To delete your account, contact us at tahsin.abc2009@gmail.com.
  • Is my data safe? Yes! Please review our Privacy Policy to understand how we protect your data.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I play Dodge-and-Collect on multiple devices?

A: Yes, by logging into your account, your progress will sync across devices.
